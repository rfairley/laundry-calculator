Optimal Laundry Spending Calculator

For students nowadays, times are tough. Even a few unused dollars left on your laundry card means a few dollars of debt you could have paid off. Luckily, a solution has been devised: the Optimal Laundry Spending Calculator. This web app finds all of the possible ways you can pay for laundry while leaving a minimal unused balance on your laundry card.

Note: Special thanks to Ninan for giving me the idea to investigate this!
